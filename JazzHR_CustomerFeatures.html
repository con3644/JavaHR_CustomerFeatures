Customer Features Assignment Page




This page assigns new features to customers. There are four groups of features. A customer has a 10% chance to be assigned to group 1, a 20% chance to be assigned to group 2, a 20% chance to be assigned to group 3 and a 50% chance to be assigned to group 4.

Customers assigned to group 1 have access to features A and B. Customers assigned to group 2 have access to features B and C. Customers assigned to group 3 have access to feature C. Customers assigned to group 4 have access to features A, C and D.
